How it Started

Right here, a mutual acquaintance of L. & V. discussed the idea of creating a wallet with Vlad. After the summit, he introduced the future co-founders to each other.

An afterparty conversation sparked the idea of Dollet, a multi-chain non-custodial wallet with integrated DeFi.

A perceptive party-goer, also top manager of a VC Fund with Numerous Projects to approached the Owner of the VC Fund to an investor, overheard their discussion about creating a wallet for a healthy lifestyle with integrated DeFi, marking the inception of a transformative idea.
